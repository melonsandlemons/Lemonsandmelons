📦 How Zero Inventory Works
🖨 Print-on-Demand (Mugs, Shirts, Totes…)
Customer orders → Printify prints & ships it directly to them → money lands in your bank. You never touch any product.
📦 Dropshipping (Pre-Made Products)
Customer orders → Zendrop ships from their warehouse directly to the customer → you keep the markup. No inventory, no packing.
📄 Digital Downloads (Printables, Planners…)
Customer pays → Etsy emails them a download link instantly → zero fulfillment cost. The same file sells forever.
🔑 The Core Principle
You collect the customer's money first. Then the production cost is deducted. You never pay out of pocket for inventory.
