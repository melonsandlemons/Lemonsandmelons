Welcome to Your Business Dashboard
This app is your personal command center for the online business you're building. Your husband set this up for you as part of a complete business system designed specifically around your life. You do not need any tech experience to use it — everything is tap-to-update and built for your iPhone, iPad, or Mac.

Here's all you need to know to get started:
1
Tap any number to update it
On the Social and Revenue screens, tap any number and type your real one. It saves automatically.
2
Update once a week
Every Friday, spend 5 minutes entering your follower counts and income from each platform.
3
Check the Tasks tab weekly
It shows a simple checklist of your 2–3 hour weekly routine. Tap each item to check it off.
4
Your data syncs live
Everything saves to Firebase in real time. Open on any device and see the same data.
5
Use the Setup Guide first
The Setup tab has a phase-by-phase checklist for getting everything started. Do Phase 1 this week.
6
Change the theme
Tap the sun icon (top right) to pick from 5 premium themes. Your choice saves automatically.
