📦 How Zero Inventory Works
🖨 Print-on-Demand (Mugs, Shirts, Totes…)
Customer orders → Printify prints & ships it directly to them → money lands in your bank. You never buy, store, or touch any product. Your only job: design it once in Canva and publish it.
📦 Dropshipping (Pre-Made Products)
Customer orders → Zendrop ships from their warehouse directly to the customer → you keep the markup. No inventory. No packing. Shopify orders auto-fulfill. Etsy orders take 3-4 min of manual entry.
📄 Digital Downloads (Printables, Planners…)
Customer pays → Etsy emails them a download link instantly → you earned money while doing nothing. Zero fulfillment cost. The same file sells forever. $0 production cost per sale, ever.
🔑 The Core Principle
You collect the customer's money first. Then the production cost is deducted. You never pay out of pocket for a product. You never buy inventory hoping it sells. Every cost is covered by the sale itself.
